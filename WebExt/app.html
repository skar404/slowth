Enable in Safari
  1. Open Settings on your device
  2. Go to Apps → Safari → Extensions
  3. Turn on Slowth and allow it on every website

Guide
What Strict mode does
Strict mode locks every toggle on this screen for 24 hours. The timer survives restarts, force-quits and reinstalling Safari. Once it's on, you can't lower it — only wait it out, or delete and reinstall the app.
Turn it on when willpower alone isn't enough. Leave it off if you just want a soft nudge.
How blocking works
Off The site loads as usual. Slowth does nothing.
Block shorts The Shorts/Reels feed, tab and ribbon are hidden. The rest of the site keeps working.
Block shorts + feed Also stops the endless home feed with a blocked page after a few screens. Facebook & Instagram only.
Block site Visiting the site shows a friendly blocked page instead.
